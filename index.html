Brought to you by: Computer Science Club

Who is Elon Musk

A famous South African/American technologist, innovator and billioniare. CEO of SpaceX, SolarCity and Tesla

Initially a software developer most notable one was PayPal itself which he sold of to eBay to earn his starting fortune. Mr. Musk has branched of far and wide into renewable energies, electric vehicles and now space exploration. His next challenge is to colonize Mars. He currently funds a non-profit OpenAI research company that promotes safe AI and ensures its uses are widely and evenly distrubuted.

Lecture topic: The Future and AI

Artificial Intelligence is the next "big" thing on the horizon. Machine Learning and AI contribtutes to numerous applications from self-driving vehicles, learning patterns and simulating traffic in a city.

Mr. Musk will talk about how AI will shape the future to mankind. What roles will it take and will it make some jobs obselete. He will explain on how does his company intergrate AI into their products and how it helps his users and its potential dangers
